candied sweet potato

Image is for illustrative purposes only

    ーIngredientsー
  • Sweet potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (if available)
  • A little salt
  • Sesame seeds

-Recipe-

  1. Mix honey, soy sauce, and salt with water to make the sauce.
  2. Put the sweet potatoes in a bowl with the skin on and soak in water.
  3. Drain off all the water, cover loosely with plastic wrap, and microwave for 5 minutes at 600W.
  4. Heat a frying pan with oil over medium heat, place the sweet potatoes in the pan after wiping them dry, and brown them well.
  5. After wiping off excess oil, add the mixed sauce and heat over low heat.
  6. When the sweet potatoes are completely soft, they are done. It tastes better if you sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
